Bardsley English Medium Higher Secondary School also known as Bardsley English Medium Higher Secondary School. The school was established in 1964. Bardsley English Medium Higher Secondary School is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It is managed by Nagpur Diocesan Board Of Education.
